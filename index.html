Elena Knapp

Elena (Prikhodko) Knapp is a calligrapher, she is a member of IAMPETH (International Association of Master Penmen, Engrossers and Teachers of Handwriting) and also of local Capital City Scribe. Elena is a self-taught artist that lives and creates in Austin, Texas, USA. Her work has been shown throughout various venues of Texas, Elena's work is represented in a number of private collections world wide including USA, Canada, Australia, Europe, Russia and Middle East.


Elena grew up in Russia and lived in the Middle East (UAE Dubai) for 14 years before moving to Austin. She lives with her husband Chris and sweet doggy Olive. Elena has always been creative and loved arts but started to paint in 2006 and started lettering and calligraphy in 2010.


Calligraphy services - Traditional Calligraphy, Trendy and Modern Envelope Addressing, Invitation Design, Menu Design, Place Cards and Escort Cards, Wedding Vows, Awards and Certificates with custom hand-painted borders and illumination, Special commissions of poetry, Song lyrics, etc. Elena creates surface patterns and always open for a discussion on licensing her work.
