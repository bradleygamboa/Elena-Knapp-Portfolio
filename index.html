About

Elena (Prikhodko) Knapp is a self-taught mixed media artist and calligrapher that lives and creates in Austin, Texas, USA Elena is Russian and before moving to Austin lived in Middle East (UAE Dubai) for 14 years. Elena has always been creative and loved arts but started to paint herself only a few years ago. Her work has been shown throughout various venues of Texas, Elena's work is represented in a number of private collections world wide including USA, Canada, Australia, Europe, Russia and MIddle East. She uses different paints such as 3D/acrylic/ceramic/glass/ink. All ornaments that Elena creates are original and never repeated. Most of her ornaments are painted in Mandala style Mandala is a Sanskrit word that means "circle". - Wikipedia.
