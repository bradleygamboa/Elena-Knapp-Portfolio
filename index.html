Elena Knapp

Hi everyone, my name is Elena (Prikhodko) Knapp and I am a calligrapher /artist. I am a member of IAMPETH (International Association of Master Penmen, Engrossers and Teachers of Handwriting) and of a local guild called Capital City Scribes (Austin Texas). I grew up in Russia and before moving to USA lived in Middle East (UAE Dubai) for 14 years. I live now in Austin Texas with my always supportive husband Chris and our dog Olive.


I have always been creative and loved the arts. I began to paint in 2006. I started lettering and Calligraphy in 2010, when I moved to Austin. My work has been shown throughout various venues of Texas, and been represented in a number of private collections world wide including USA, Canada, Australia, Europe, Russia and MIddle East.


I do provide a variety of Calligraphy services - Traditional Calligraphy & Trendy, Modern, Envelope Addressing, Invitation Design, Menu Design, Place Cards & Escort Cards, Wedding Vows, awards and certificates with custom hand-painted borders and illumination, special commissions of poetry, song lyrics, etc. I also work on creating surface patterns and always open for a discussion on licensing my work.


Please feel free to email me to start your project.

Looking forward to working with you,

Elena
